The story

Sport equipment to protect the person wearing it, before any sport had any protection there would be many injuries leaving long term injuries in some cases making the player unable to continue the sport or even making them unable to continue their life in a normal way. Safety is right now the number one priority when playing a sport, when having a compition or when you're training you're always required to wear safety equipment.

What does it solve exactly?

Not only does it solve the problem of injuries it also has sensors that measure the way the player plays. So that the player can look back on how they've played and juries can look back if the player had cheated in any kind of way by looking at the statistics. Sadly the equipment doesn't prevent all injuries but most of the time it does. There is also less chances of having a long term injuries.

How to use it?

There are many different types of equipment, some are specially designed for a specific sport and some are universal meaning they can be used for many different kinds of sports. To use them you have to follow the instructions that you get when you're buying the equipment, or you could ask your trainer/coach to help you with using and wearing the equipment so you can make sure you're doing it right for you not to have any injuries.
